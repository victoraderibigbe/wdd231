Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my artwork?

Fill out the contact form above with "Artist Submission" as the subject. Include details about your work, medium, and attach images if possible. We'll review your submission and get back to you within 5-7 business days.

Is there a fee to display my art?

No, there is no fee for artists to have their work featured on CanvasNova. We believe in supporting local artists and making art accessible to everyone.

Can I purchase artwork directly?

Yes! Contact us with details about the piece you're interested in, and we'll connect you directly with the artist to discuss pricing and availability.

Do you organize exhibitions?

We regularly organize both virtual and physical exhibitions featuring our artists. Follow our newsletter to stay updated on upcoming events and exhibitions.
