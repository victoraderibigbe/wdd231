Discover Local Artists - Site Plan

1. Site Name

CanvasNova

This name was selected because it combines "Canvas", symbolizing the medium of artistic expression, with "Nova", a term that represents a new star or a brilliant burst of light. CanvasNova captures the idea of new and rising creativityâ€”a platform where emerging artists can shine and showcase their work to the world. The name conveys inspiration, innovation, and a fresh beginning in the art world. It's memorable, artistic, and clearly communicates the site's purpose of spotlighting talented local creators.

2. Site Purpose

CanvasNova serves as a comprehensive platform for discovering and connecting with emerging local artists across various creative mediums. The site provides a curated digital gallery that showcases artworks from local talent, including digital art, acrylic paintings, photography, and sculpture. Visitors can explore artist profiles, view high-quality artwork thumbnails, filter content by artistic medium, and access detailed information about each artist's background and creative process.

The platform bridges the gap between local artists seeking exposure and art enthusiasts looking to discover fresh, authentic talent in their community. Additionally, the site facilitates direct communication through a contact form, enabling potential collaborations, art submissions, and inquiries about purchasing or commissioning artwork.

3. Scenarios

The following scenarios represent common questions and needs of our target audience:

  • Art Collector/Buyer: "I'm looking for unique local artwork to purchase for my home. Can I find contact information for artists whose work I'm interested in and see examples of their portfolio?"
  • Interior Designer: "I need to find local artists who specialize in large-scale acrylic paintings that would complement my client's modern living space. How can I filter artworks by medium and size?"
  • Gallery Owner: "I'm curating an exhibition featuring emerging local artists. Where can I find detailed artist profiles and high-resolution images of their work to consider for my show?"
  • Art Student/Enthusiast: "I'm interested in learning about different artistic techniques and styles. Can I discover local artists working in various mediums and learn about their creative processes?"

5. Typography

The typography selection balances professionalism with artistic flair, ensuring excellent readability while maintaining visual interest:

Headings: Arial/Helvetica (Sans-serif)

Clean, modern sans-serif font used for all headings (H1-H4), navigation links, and button text. Provides excellent readability and a contemporary feel.

Body Text: Georgia/Times New Roman (Serif)

Classic serif font used for body text, artist descriptions, and longer content blocks. Offers excellent readability for extended reading and adds a touch of elegance and tradition to complement the artistic theme.

Font Applications:

  • Arial/Helvetica: Site title, main navigation, section headings, button labels, and form labels
  • Georgia/Times New Roman: Body paragraphs, artist descriptions, contact form content, and footer text

Technical Implementation Notes

The following technical features will be implemented to meet project requirements:

  • Data Fetching: JSON file containing artist profiles with async/await and try-catch error handling
  • Dynamic Content: Generate 15+ artist profiles with name, medium, bio, and contact information
  • Local Storage: Save user's favorite artists for future visits
  • Modal Dialogs: Detailed artist information and artwork viewing
  • Array Methods: Filter artists by medium, map data for display
  • ES Modules: Separate modules for data handling, UI components, and utilities
